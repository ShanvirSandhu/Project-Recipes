Strawberry Cheesecake

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar(crust)
  • 1 pound softened cream cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup strawberry preserves or jam
  • Whipped cream for garnish

Steps to make:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F(163 degrees C)
  2. In a bowl combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  3. Press the mixture into a 9-inch spring form pan in order to form the crust
  4. Blend in the sour cream then beat the eggs in one at a time. DO NOT OVER MIX
  5. Pour the cream cheese filling over the crust in the spring form pan
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 50 minutes to 1 hour or until the center is set
  7. While cheese cake cools, heat the strawberry perserves in a sauce pan over low heat until they become runny
  8. Arrange the strawberry slices on top of the cheese cake and then glaze with the strawberry preserves
  9. Chill the cheesecake in teh fridge for at least 4 hours
  10. Before serving garnish with whipped cream and enjoy!
