Pizza

Description:

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tea spoon garlic powder
  • 1 tea spoon dried oregano
  • 1 tea spoon dried basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour(for dusting)

Steps to make

  1. Preheat oven 475 degrees F(245 degrees C)
  2. Dust a surface with flour and stretch and role out pizza dough until it has a 12 inch diameter and create a thick rim for the crust
  3. Transfer dough to parchment paper
  4. Brush surface of dough with olive oul and then spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough
  5. Sprinkle dry ingredients on top
  6. Transfer pizza to the pre heated oven
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes
  8. Remove and let it cool then enjoy!
